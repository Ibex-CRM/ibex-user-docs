## Getting Started

### Logging In

Getting started with Ibex is as easy as 1-2-3! Here's how to access your dashboard:

1. Navigate to [Ibex Dashboard](https://ibex.goatagency.com/dashboard)
2. Click 'Login with Okta'
3. Enter your WPP Okta credentials (same as your computer login)
4. Complete the 2FA verification if prompted
5. Contact your administrator if you need help with access

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important:** If you're having trouble logging in, don't hesitate to reach out to your administrator. We're here to help!
</Callout>

### Dashboard Overview

Welcome to your command center! The main dashboard is your gateway to all Ibex applications and services. It's mission control for campaign management.
